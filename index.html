📖 Abbreviation Key

PDH/PDL/PDC – Previous Day High/Low/Close
CPR – Central Pivot Range (BC, Pivot, TC)
Pivot – (PDH + PDL + PDC) / 3
BC – Bottom Central = (PDH + PDL) / 2
TC – Top Central = 2 × Pivot − BC
Width – TC − BC, as % of Pivot
Narrow – Width < 0.15% · Wide – > 0.50%
Gap Up/Down – Open > PDH / < PDL
Range Day – |close vs open| < 0.25%
Trend Day – |close vs open| > 0.50%
Sustain – Price held beyond level after first touch
Tool was right – Predicted bullish/bearish matched actual
